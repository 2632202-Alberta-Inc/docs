## Overview

A regulatory system refers to the reporting platform or framework used for regulatory compliance within a jurisdiction. For example, Petrinex is the system used in Western Canada for oil and gas production reporting, while other regions may use different systems to facilitate similar reporting requirements. [Learn more about regulatory systems](/models/regulatory/system)
