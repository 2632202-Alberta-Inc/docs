## Overview

A regulatory system refers to the reporting platform or framework used for regulatory compliance within a jurisdiction. Regulatory systems provide standardized methods for reporting production, managing licenses, and maintaining regulatory compliance. Each jurisdiction typically has a designated system that companies must use to meet their reporting obligations.
