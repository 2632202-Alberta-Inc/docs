---
title: "Ticket Code"
---

## Overview

Ticket codes identify the type of custody transfer or inventory movement represented by a ticket. These standardized codes help categorize and track different kinds of volume measurements and transfers throughout the production and transportation process, enabling accurate accounting, reconciliation, and reporting.

## Attributes

<ResponseField name="INV">
  Inventory - Used for tracking product inventory levels and movements within a
  facility, such as tank gauging and storage measurements.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="BLEND">
  Product blend - Indicates mixing or blending of different streams or qualities
  in storage tanks or processing units. Primarily used in blending operations.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="INJ">
  Injected product - Used when product is being injected into facility equipment
  or systems, such as storage tanks or processing units. Primarily used in
  blending operations.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="DISP">
  Disposition - Indicates final disposition and custody transfer of product to
  pipelines, trucks, or other transportation methods.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="LOCAL">
  Local transfer - Used for product movements between batteries, satellites, and
  processing facilities within the same operating area, where the product is not
  held in custody of the commercial entity.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="REC">
  Received product - Indicates volumes received at a facility via pipeline,
  truck, or from producing wells.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="SHIP">
  Shipped product - Indicates volumes shipped from a facility via pipeline or
  truck to downstream facilities.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="SHIP_INV">
  Shipped inventory - Used for reconciling shipped volumes against inventory.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="SALE">
  Sales point - Indicates custody transfer measurements at designated sales
  points to third parties.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="PRE_INV">
  Inventory entrance point - Used for measurements taken before product enters
  inventory.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="POST_INV">
  Inventory exit point - used for measurements taken after product leaves
  inventory
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="IN_OUT">
  Intra-facility transfers - General inbound/outbound movement between
  facilities
</ResponseField>
